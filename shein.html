Pull&Bear specializes in manufacturing and selling urban style clothing and accessories.[3] The brand uses the US popular culture in its product design. New product lines introduced in stores have diversified the range of products available in Pull&Bear outlets. These new lines include; music, technology, video games and video images mixed in with the clothing. Pull&Bear introduced the "XDYE" line in 1998, a more sporty and hi-tech line of clothes linked to the icons of 21st-century youth culture.
